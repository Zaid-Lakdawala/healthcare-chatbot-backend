EVALUATION SCORING RUBRIC
===========================================

For each response, score 1-5 on each criterion:

CONTEXT (1-5):
5 - Perfectly maintains conversation context
4 - Good context awareness with minor gaps
3 - Adequate context, some inconsistencies
2 - Poor context awareness
1 - Completely ignores context

RELEVANCY (1-5):
5 - Highly relevant to user's query
4 - Mostly relevant with minor deviations
3 - Somewhat relevant
2 - Barely relevant
1 - Completely irrelevant

ACCURACY (1-5):
5 - Medically accurate based on available knowledge
4 - Mostly accurate with minor issues
3 - Partially accurate
2 - Mostly inaccurate
1 - Completely inaccurate or dangerous

COHERENCE (1-5):
5 - Perfectly logical and well-structured
4 - Mostly logical with minor issues
3 - Somewhat logical
2 - Confusing or unclear
1 - Incoherent or nonsensical

TOTAL SCORE: Sum of all criteria (Max: 20 per response)
OVERALL SCORE: Average across all 100 prompts
